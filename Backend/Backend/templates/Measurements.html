📐 How to Take Measurements
  • Chest: Measure around the fullest part of the chest, keeping tape horizontal.
  • Waist: Measure around your natural waistline, keeping tape comfortably loose.
  • Length: From shoulder top to desired hemline (shirt/kurta length).
  • Shoulder: From one shoulder edge to the other across the back.
  • Hip: Around the fullest part of your hips, approx 8 inches below waist.
  • Sleeve: From shoulder edge to wrist with arm slightly bent.
  • Neck: Around the base of the neck for collar fitting.
  • Inseam: From crotch to ankle for trouser/pant fitting.
